Add Veteran Archives to Your Phone

Veteran Archives runs as an app right from your phone's Home Screen — full-screen, no address bar — so it's easy to use out in the cemeteries.

iPhone & iPad

Apple devices
Veteran Archives isn't available through the Apple App Store at this time as a free, civic, web-based tool. You can still add it as an app to your Apple device by following these few steps:
  1. Open Safari and go to markwot-ops.github.io/Veteran-Archives
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow pointing up, at the bottom-center of the screen.
  3. Scroll down the menu and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add in the top-right corner.
  5. Close Safari and open the new Vet Archives icon from your Home Screen.

Android

Samsung, Google Pixel, and other Android phones
  1. Open Chrome and go to markwot-ops.github.io/Veteran-Archives
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top-right).
  3. Tap Install app (or Add to Home screen).
  4. Tap Install / Add to confirm.
